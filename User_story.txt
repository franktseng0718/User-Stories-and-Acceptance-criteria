Uber new function
User story:
As a disabled I want to book a car so that I can place a wheelchair

Acceptance criteria:
GIVEN: Disabled navigate to setting page
WHEN: User click "choose a trip" and choose car to disabled Uber
THEN: Enable disabled to choose the welcab or a van
GIVEN: Disabled choose the welcab with setting "choose a trip"
WHEN: Disabled navigate to the place to take the car
THEN: Enable disabled to take a welcab
